How many laws are there in Indian Constitution?

Constitution of India contains 395 articles in 22 parts. Additional articles and parts are inserted later through various amendments. There are also 12 schedules in the Indian Constitution.

What is Act and Rule?

An act is a law or the statute which has been passed by the legislature and approved by the President of India. Rules, on the other hand, help in governing law. They are secondary. They are in place to make the parent Act work effectivel
